DSCA Program Goal: Employer-validated curriculum that drives measurable learner outcomes and labor-market placement

Signal → Curriculum Decision → Delivery → Outcome. Both engines required. Neither dominates.

Engine 1
External Market Signal
Employer Engagement
Advisor NPS
Net Promoter Score from DSCA employer advisors post-session
Target: ≥ 70 Source: DSCA survey Per cycle
Advisor Participation Rate
% of invited advisors who complete a session in the period
Target: ≥ 80% Source: Airtable Quarterly
Macro Coverage
% of active domains with ≥1 completed Macro advisor session
Target: 100% by Q3 Source: Airtable Monthly
Signal Alignment
Curriculum Alignment Score
Advisor-rated match between current curriculum and employer skill needs (1–5 scale)
Target: ≥ 4.0 Source: DSCA session Per cycle
Micro Coverage
% of target courses with completed Micro (skill/competency-level) advisor capture
Target: ≥ 60% by Day 60 Source: Airtable Monthly
Engine 2
Internal Feedback Loop
Signal Volume & Capture
Feedback Submission Rate
% of active courses with ≥1 internal signal submitted per cohort (IDQA + LEAD + Canvas)
Target: ≥ 85% Source: Airtable / App Per cohort
Signal Triage SLA
% of Engine 2 signals triaged (accept / defer / decline) within 48 hours of submission
Target: 100% Source: Asana log Weekly
Signal Quality & Action
Change Criticality Index
Weighted score of accepted signals by severity (Minor / Moderate / Critical) — tracks urgency of backlog
Watch threshold: >3 Critical open Source: Airtable Monthly
Close-the-Loop Rate
% of accepted signals with a documented response sent back to the originating source
Target: ≥ 90% Source: Airtable Quarterly
Pattern-Trigger Rate
% of Engine 2 patterns (≥2 cohorts) that result in a Phase 0 refresh intake
Target: 100% of qualifying patterns Source: Asana + Airtable Quarterly
Program Health
Dashboard & Outcomes
Signal-to-Action Conversion
Signal-to-Refresh Rate
% of accepted signals (E1 + E2 combined) that result in a documented curriculum decision within 90 days
Target: ≥ 75% Source: Asana decision log Quarterly
Market Validation Coverage
% of net-new or major refreshes with documented DSCA employer validation evidence (SOP gate requirement)
Target: 100% Source: Asana Phase 2 gate Per launch
Learner & Placement Outcomes
Credential Attainment Rate
% of learners in DSCA-validated programs earning the target credential
Threshold: ≥ 70% (Salesforce trigger) Source: Salesforce Per cohort
Job Attainment Rate (JAR)
% of DSCA-validated program graduates placed in a role within 6 months
Benchmark: org baseline + trend Source: Salesforce Quarterly
Program Ops
Dashboard Live Date
Date a live KPI dashboard is accessible to Bolaji / product team (milestone, not ongoing)
Target: Day 60 (≈ Jul 6) Source: Airtable / TBD One-time
