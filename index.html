🎯

DSCA turns employer + delivery signal into curriculum decisions, with a paper trail.

One closed loop. Two engines feed it. Airtable is the system of record. Engine 1 captures what the market wants before we build. Engine 2 captures what's actually happening after we launch. Together they govern every curriculum change.

Systems We Operate In
Airtable — System of Record
Single source of truth. Intake → recommendations → decisions → implementation tracking. "If it's not in Airtable, it didn't happen." Holds: DSCA Intake, Advisor Pool, Survey Tracker, Session Log, Scoring Layer, G5 Decision Log, Close-the-Loop Tracker, IFF Feedback Tracker, PCRA Results, NPS Log, Retros, Course Version Registry, KPI Dashboard.
Salesforce
Outcomes layer. Job Attainment Rate (JAR), credential attainment, cohort completion, employer satisfaction. Source for placement and outcome KPIs.
Asana
Work tracking + decision-log tracking + Phase 2 gate evidence + triage SLA tracking. Engine 2 release work breakdown lives here.
Canvas
